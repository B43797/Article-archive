Goldbach's Conjecture Validated up to 1010000

This website provides a public explanation and downloadable archive based on the GPS method developed by Bahbouhi Bouchaib. This method allows decomposing any even number E > 4 into two primes p and q such that E = p + q, with validation up to 1010000.
